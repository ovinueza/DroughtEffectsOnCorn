Importance of Corn in the United States

1999 - States pass bans on MTBE. These bans increased the need for ethanol as they went into effect.

2005 to 2015 - Large increases in the usage of ethanol in the US due to the Renewable Fuel Standard requiring 10% ethanol usage in gasoline.
2019 - In the past year, almost 15 billion gallons of ethanol is blended into gasoline in the U.S.
